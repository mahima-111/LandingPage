ABOUT JAPAN

Japan is an island country in East Asia. It is situated in the northwest Pacific Ocean and is bordered on the west by the Sea of Japan, extending from the Sea of Okhotsk in the north toward the East China Sea, Philippine Sea, and Taiwan in the south.

What is Japan's best known for?

Japan is most famous for its cultural heritage, traditional art, ceremonial rituals such as tea ceremonies; calligraphy, origami and unique style of flower arrangements known as Ikebana. Japan is also famous for its beautifully landscaped gardens, poetry, sculpture and more than a dozen UNESCO World Heritage sites.

What is the best time to visit Japan?

March to May and October to November is the best time to visit Japan. During these months you can enjoy the best weather in Japan and also indulge in various festivals including the world famous Cherry Blossom Festival.
